ProseMirror Comments Example

Two styles of editor are shown here:

  • A Peer Review editor which allows commenting but not editing. Swipe the text and click on the comment icon to add a comment.
  • An Editor with Suggestions which allows peers to make suggestions for inserting, deleting, replacing or commenting.
    The author may then approve or reject the suggestions.
